If you want to look up a job, you need to run this page in Chrome and have this extension installed.
1.) Click on add to Chrome then click the button on the top right of the window that says CORS.
2.) In the INTERCEPTED URLs or URL patterns, you’re going to delete the one that says “*://*/*”
3.) Replace instead with: https://api.smartsheet.com/*, the https is important as well as the * wildcard.
4.) Once that’s done, click Enable cross-origin resource sharing, and carry on!
